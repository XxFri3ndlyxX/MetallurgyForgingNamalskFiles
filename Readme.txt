Open regular.namalsk folder.

Drag and drop EditorFiles folder into your regular.namalsk or hardcore.namalsk mission folder.

You need DayZ Editor Loader to be able to load DZE files which are the Lead and Iron cave.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2276010135
If you do not install this, some of those boulders will float in the air.
You will also need builder item for the item needed. 
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1565871491&searchtext=builderitem
If you have expansion then builder item is not necessary.


Next open AddThisToYour cfgeventspawns.xml copy content to your mission cfgeventspawns.xml

then open AddThisToYourEvents.xml and copy content to your mission/db events.xml
